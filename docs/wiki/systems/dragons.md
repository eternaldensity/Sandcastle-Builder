# Dragons

> Source: [Dragons](https://xkcd-time.fandom.com/wiki/Dragons)

**Dragons** are creatures in the game [[Sandcastle_Builder|Sandcastle Builder]]. They play a role in the high endgame, way beyond the infinite glass stage. When you have 50 maps, you can buy for 200M [[boosts/Bonemeal|Bonemeal]] (80M with ASHF) the [[boosts/Dragon Nesting Site|Dragon Nesting Site]]. This will unlock [[boosts/Dragon Nest|Dragon Nest]]. This needs 5T [[boosts/Blackprints|Blackprints]] and uses 50T runs of AA to complete. Once completed, this can unlock [[boosts/Dragon Queen|Dragon Queen]] if you have AC >= 1M and at least 1G [[boosts/Bonemeal|Bonemeal]]. When the [[boosts/Dragon Queen|Dragon Queen]] is bought, it unlocks [[boosts/Raptorish Dragon Keeping Manual|Raptorish Dragon Keeping Manual]], that has a lot of information about dragons. You can change the lining of the nest, to determine how your dragon will look like. The first egg costs 1M [[boosts/Bonemeal|Bonemeal]]. If you want to lay another egg at the same time, it costs 1W [[boosts/Bonemeal|Bonemeal]].

There are two stages:

- Hatching means breeding the egg (takes 2000 mNP).
- Then you have to feed the dragon 1M goats. If you don't do this fast enough your hatchlings will die.
- Then after another 2000 mNP, the dragon is ready to fledge. This means that it gets it a place on a certain NP. It will have to fight against another creature. If you set a dragon on a NP that has already dragon's, the strongest will kill the weakest, so don't do that.

## Linings of the nest

You can give your nest certain linings. They have to add up to 100%. You need to have an infinite amount of the stuff you choose. You can choose a n% per stuff category.

- [[boosts/Sand|Sand]] and [[boosts/Castles|Castles]] increase Offense.
- Glass Chips and Blocks increase Defense.
- [[boosts/Blackprints|Blackprints]] and [[boosts/Flux Crystals|Flux Crystals]] increase digging.

It is strongly recommended that your first dragon has 50% offense and 50% defense. When you have boosts that increase offense and defense, you can work on digging.

## Outcomes of the fight

A fight can have several outcomes: Totally destroyed, Lost with dignity, Tie, Very hard victory, Hard victory and Easy victory. In the beginning, you gain +0.2 [[boosts/Experience|Experience]] of *totally destroyed*and +1 [[boosts/Experience|Experience]] of *lost with dignity.* victory gives you 2 [[boosts/Experience|Experience]] and some Copper (which is a millionth of a piece of [[boosts/Gold|Gold]]). [[boosts/Experience|Experience]] is needed to get a new level of the [[boosts/Dragon Queen|Dragon Queen]], which get you stronger types of dragons and better boosts. Redundaknights can come instead of redundakitties, they fight with the dragons as well.

## Digging

Dragons can dig. They can find [[boosts/Gold|Gold]] and [[boosts/Diamonds|Diamonds]], but in the beginning you'll usually find 1 Copper. Digging can be increased by lining the nest more for [[boosts/Blackprints|Blackprints]] and [[boosts/Flux Crystals|Flux Crystals]] and several Boosts.
