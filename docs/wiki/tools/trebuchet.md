# Trebuchet

> Source: [Trebuchet](https://xkcd-time.fandom.com/wiki/Trebuchet)

The first evidence of the trebuchet was seen in [[Frame|Frame]] 212, when the first projectile was sighted. The trebuchet itself was first seen in Frame 217, as [[Megan|Megan]] dragged it closer to the sandcastles, becoming entirely visible in Frame 218. As of 239, 6 visible projectiles have been fired by the trebuchet.
