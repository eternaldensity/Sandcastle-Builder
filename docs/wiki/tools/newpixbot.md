# NewPixBot

> Source: [Newpixbot](https://xkcd-time.fandom.com/wiki/Newpixbot)

Introduced in [this post](http://forums.xkcd.com/viewtopic.php?f=7&t=101043&start=14840#p3333560) by mscha, the Newpixbot is a forum bot/script responsible for posting the [[ONG|ONG]] in the case that no user is online to do so, or everyone forgets. It was created as a backup in case of emergency, and received some backlash after some users thought it would stifle the friendly competition of posting the new frame. Thus, it was updated first to wait for [5 minutes](http://forums.xkcd.com/viewtopic.php?f=7&t=101043&start=15000#p3333986), then for [10 minutes](http://forums.xkcd.com/viewtopic.php?f=7&t=101043&start=15000#p3334094) before doing so.

Since its creation, the newpixbot was the inspiration for a variety of [[Derivative_Works|derivative works]] including a [[Tarot_Cards|tarot card]]. It is also counted separately from mscha on [Kieryn's data page](http://xkcd-time.kieryn.com/), and has been known to submit images and once even a filk, seemingly of its own accord.
